Our Story

Rooted in Karaikudi.
Cooking for Chennai.

Ponni began in 2012 in a small rented kitchen in T. Nagar. Founder Meenakshi Rajan had one goal — to cook exactly the way her mother did in Karaikudi, and share it with people who had never tasted real Chettinad food.

She brought her mother's stone grinder, her grandmother's clay pots, and fifteen years of watching, learning and tasting. No recipe books. Just memory, love and stubbornness to never take shortcuts.

"We don't cook food. We cook memories. And memories don't take shortcuts."
— Meenakshi Rajan, Founder & Head Chef
